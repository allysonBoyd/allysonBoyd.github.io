Claire Wineland: Positive & beautiful as always

Claire spent most of her life in the hospital. She was diagnosed with Cystic Fibrosis as a baby, but that didn't stop her from living a normal life. Claire says this was her only problem with having cf "We cannot keep teaching people who are sick that they need to be healed before they can live their lives." She shead light on things that most people don't think about. She explained that "The quality of your life is'nt determined by weather you are healthy or sick or rich or poor. It's determined by what you make of your expeirinces as a human being." And even thoughy Claire peacfully pased she is still living in our hearts. She was an amazing beautiful soul!

  • - She performed with Philips "Breathless Choir"
  • August 26 2018: - A week after the transplant surgery, Claire suffered a stroke due to a blood clot which cut off the blood supply to the right side of her brain.
  • September 2: - On September 2, 2018, she passed away peacefully. Her organs and tissue were donated to help give others time to share their gifts.
  • After - After Claires passing, a celebration of life was held and "Claire's Place Foundation" kept on.
